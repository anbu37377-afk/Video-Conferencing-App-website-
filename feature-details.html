Frequently Asked Questions

How secure are NexusMeet meetings?

All meetings are encrypted using AES-256 and SRTP. We use end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest.

Can I record meetings for later viewing?

Yes, you can record to the cloud or locally. Cloud recordings include automatic AI-generated transcripts.

What is the maximum number of participants?

Our Enterprise plan supports up to 1,000 active participants and 50,000 viewers for webinars.
